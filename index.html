Easily convert between coordinate formats and visualize them on a map with KoordKonvert. Whether you're a hiker, surveyor, military personnel or geospatial enthusiast, this app simplifies working with coordinates like Decimal Degrees (DD), Decimal Degrees Minutes (DDM), Degrees Minutes Seconds (DMS), UTM (Universal Transverse Mercator), MGRS (Military Grid Reference System), and even regional systems like EOV (Hungary) - and more is coming soon.

Key Features:

Coordinate Conversion: Transform coordinates between DD, DDM, DMS, UTM, MGRS, and EOV with precision and OFFLINE.

Interactive Maps: View your coordinates on OpenStreetMap or unlock Google Maps (Normal, Satellite, Hybrid, Terrain) for detailed visualization.

Get Your Location: Use GPS to input your current coordinates instantly.

Save & Manage: Store your favorite coordinates with timestamps or custom names for quick access.

Copy & Share: Copy converted coordinates to your clipboard with one tap.

Multilingual Support: Available in English, Hungarian, German, Spanish, Polish, and Italian.

Customizable Settings: Toggle input assistant, regional systems, map types, and keep-screen-on options.

Ad-Free Option: Upgrade to remove ads and unlock Google Maps permanently.

How It Works:

1. Enter or fetch your coordinates using GPS.

2. Select input and output formats from the dropdowns.

3. Convert and view results instantly—or show them on a map!

4. Copy or save your coordinates for later use.
